Plastic Kettle

Items Needed

  • A Plastic Bottle
  • Scissors
  • Hot Glue Gun
  • A Thin Tool (Possibly A Screwdriver Or Similar Pointed Object)

How This Product Can Help

Reusing plastic bottles can reduce plastic waste, which is beneficial for the environment. Plastic waste contributes to climate change and pollutes water bodies. Reusing plastic bottles reduces the demand for new plastic production, which requires oil and gas and emits carbon into the atmosphere.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • The bottom part of the plastic bottle is cut off using scissors.
  • The cut edge of the remaining bottle is folded inwards and secured with hot glue.
  • A small triangular piece is cut out from the side of the bottle near the top.This will form the spout of the kettle.
  • A strip of plastic is cut from the discarded bottom part of the bottle.
  • This strip is then curved and attached to the side of the bottle using hot glue to create a handle.
  • The resulting object resembles a small kettle that can pour liquid.

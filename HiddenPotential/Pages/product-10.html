Plastic Travel Bottle

Items Needed

  • Plastic Bottle
  • Knife
  • Candle
  • Lighter
  • Marker

How this product helps

The DIY plastic travel container offers a practical, personalized, and protective solution for organizing small items, particularly useful when on the go.

  • Start by thoroughly washing and drying your chosen plastic bottle, both inside and out. It's crucial to remove all labels and any sticky residue, leaving you with a clean, clear base for your project.

    • Mark Your Cutting Lines

  • First, decide on the overall length you want your finished travel container to be. Then, measure up from the bottom of the bottle to mark the cutting line.
  • Carefully cut along the marked line and remember, safety is paramount: always cut away from your body and ensure that you're working on a stable surface.
  • Heat (Use Extreme Caution!): You can briefly and very carefully pass the cut edge over a low flame (like from a candle or lighter). The heat will slightly melt and dull the edge. Perform this very quickly (just a second or two), in a well-ventilated area, and be extremely careful not to ignite or significantly deform the plastic. It's wise to practice on a scrap piece first so you'll get the hang of it when you smooth the edges with more caution.

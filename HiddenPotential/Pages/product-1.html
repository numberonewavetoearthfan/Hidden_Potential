Recycled Plastic Piggy Bank

Items Needed

  • Plastic Bottle
  • Paint
  • Craft Paper
  • Googly Eyes
  • Bottle Caps
  • Glue
  • Scissors
  • Marker

How This Product Can Help

Turning discarded plastic models into a functional Piglet piggy bank is a small but meaningful step towards a more sustainable lifestyle. It exemplifies the "reduce, reuse, recycle" principle, giving waste a second life and reducing its environmental burden.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • Prepare the Bottle: Remove any labels and clean the bottle thoroughly.
  • Paint the Bottle: Apply pink paint to the entire bottle and let it dry completely.
  • Create the Coin Slot: Using a craft knife, carefully cut a rectangular slot on the top side of the bottle for inserting coins.r
  • Make the Nose: Paint the bottle's cap pink and draw two black nostrils with a marker.
  • Add Eyes: Glue googly eyes above the nose area.
  • Attach the Legs: Glue four bottle caps to the bottom of the bottle to serve as legs.

    • Craft the Ears and Tail:

  • Ears: Cut two ear shapes from pink craft paper and glue them near the top of the bottle.
  • Tail: Cut a thin strip of pink paper, curl it to form a tail, and attach it to the back.

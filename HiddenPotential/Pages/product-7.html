DIY Plastic Bottle Organizer

Items Needed

  • Plastic Bottles
  • Scissors
  • Cardboard
  • Hot Glue
  • Decorative Materials

How this product helps

A DIY plastic bottle organizer is a practical solution that reduces your carbon footprint, conserves resources, and directly combats plastic pollution, all while promoting a more sustainable approach to consumption.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • Thoroughly wash and dry all plastic bottles, inside and out. Remove all labels. Ensure there's no sticky residue.

    • Determine Heights and Mark

  • Decide on the desired height for each compartment of your organizer. You might want different heights for different items (e.g., taller for pens, shorter for paper clips).
  • Use a marker and ruler to draw a straight line around each bottle at your chosen height.

    • Cut The Bottles

  • Carefully cut each bottle along your marked line.
  • When finished discard the top portions of the bottles (or save them for other crafts!).

    • Arrange Your Compartments

  • Place your cut bottle pieces together on a flat surface in the arrangement you desire for your organizer. You can line them up, create a cluster, or form a specific shape. Consider the items you want to put in them.

    • Create a Support Stick for The Organizer & The Base

  • Apply hot glue onto the cardboard measured with your desired height and roll it till it becomes a cylinder rigid enough to support the plastic bottles.
  • Cut another cardboard into a cirlce for the base of the plastic organizer to stick the support stick onto it.

    • Assemble With Hot Glue

  • Once you have your arrangement, start gluing the bottles and the support stick together.
  • Apply a line of hot glue along the touching edges of two bottles. Press them firmly together for about 10-15 seconds until the glue sets.
  • Continue gluing additional bottles to the arrangement until your organizer is complete. Ensure all connections are secure. Let the glue cool completely. Then, there you have it! You can style it as you like with the decorative materials or follow the youtube tutorial's instructions using colored paper to decorate the cardboards!

DIY Recycled Plastic Mug

Items Needed

  • Plastic Bottle
  • Hot Glue
  • Knife
  • Scissors
  • Marker

How this product helps

Upcycling plastic bottles transforms potential waste into useful items, directly contributing to a cleaner planet and more sustainable practices.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • Prepare the Bottle: Remove any labels and clean the bottle thoroughly.
  • Determine Height: Decide how tall you want your holder to be. Mark a line around the bottle with your marker.
  • Create the Coin Slot: Using a craft knife, carefully cut a rectangular slot on the top side of the bottle for inserting coins.r
  • Cut the Bottle: Carefully cut the bottle along your marked line. For thicker plastic or intricate cuts, a craft knife might be easier, but be very cautious. For thinner plastic, strong scissors work well.

    • Create a Handle

  • Cut a strip of plastic from another part of the same bottle, or a different bottle. Shape it into a handle. The size and shape are up to you.
  • Apply hot glue to both ends of your plastic handle piece. Firmly press the handle onto the side of your "mug," holding it in place until the glue sets. You might need to reinforce it with more glue.

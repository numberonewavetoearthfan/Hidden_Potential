Items Needed

  • A Plastic Bottle
  • A Marker
  • Scissors
  • Paint
  • Brush

How This Product Can Help

This video demonstrates a simple and creative way to transform discarded plastic bottles into adorable animal-themed planters. By cutting and painting the bottles, you can create unique homes for your small plants, resembling cute animals like cats, bunnies, and pandas. These upcycled planters add a touch of whimsy to any space and are perfect for small succulents or other indoor plants.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • Draw the outline of the animal ears on the side of a clean, empty plastic bottle using a dry erase marker.
  • Cut along the lines you've drawn to create the ear shapes at the top of the bottle.
  • Wipe off the dry erase marker lines.
  • Paint the outside of the bottle with your chosen base color using a brush.
  • Let the paint dry completely.
  • Use marker to draw the animal's facial features, such as eyes, nose, and whiskers. You can also add color to the inside of the ears.
  • Once the marker is dry, place a plant inside your new animal planter.

DIY Craft Apron from Bag

Items Needed

  • Plastic Bag
  • Tape
  • Scissors
  • Ribbons

How This Product Helps

An apron made from recycled plastic bags isn't just a functional item; it's a visible symbol of environmental stewardship, contributing to a reduction in waste, energy consumption, and pollution, while promoting a more sustainable approach to resource management.

Step By Step Tutorial

  • Prepare the Bag: Lay the plastic bag flat and cut open the sides, leaving the bottom seam intact to create a large rectangular sheet.
  • Shape the Apron: Cut the top corners to form armholes and a curved neckline, resembling a traditional apron shape.
  • Attach the Neck Strap: Tape a ribbon or string to the top corners to create a loop that fits over your head.
  • Add Waist Ties: Tape ribbons or strings to the sides at waist level for tying around your back.

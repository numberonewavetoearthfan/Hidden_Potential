Plastic Cable Organizer

Items Needed

  • A Plastic Bottle
  • Scissors

How This Product Can Help

This DIY project creates a compact and organized storage solution for various electronic wires and adapters. The modified bottle neck pieces act as individual spools to keep the wires neatly wrapped and prevent tangling, while the larger bottle body serves as a container for the adapter blocks.

Step-by-Step Tutorial

  • Remove the labels from the plastic bottles.
  • Unscrew the caps from the bottles.
  • Using scissors, carefully cut off the bottom part of each plastic bottle, leaving only the neck and the cap.
  • Take one of the cut bottle necks and make several vertical cuts around the open edge using scissors.
  • Fold these cut pieces outwards to create small flaps.
  • Screw this modified bottle neck onto another bottle cap.
  • Repeat the process to create multiple such connected pieces.
  • Wrap the wires of your adapters around these connected bottle neck pieces.
  • Place the adapters inside a larger, uncut plastic bottle for storage.
  • ou can then place the wrapped wires with their connectors into the modified bottle neck pieces attached to the cap of the larger bottle.
